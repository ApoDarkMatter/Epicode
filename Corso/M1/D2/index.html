Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. lt will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Featured in:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes Taht Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on the top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
